Privacy

JS Hero does not track or collect any personal information. Neither cookies nor tools like Google Analytics are used. Entered solutions are stored locally, i.e. on the device of the respective user. The JS Hero web application logs every page call anonymously. Anonymized means that the requesting IP address is not logged. The JS Hero Android app does not collect any data.
